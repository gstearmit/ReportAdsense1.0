Restore Master PIN Hide

You will have 1 opportunity to change your Master PIN at no charge, and you will be charged 2 USD for every further attempt. If your account funds are not suffiicient, you will not be able to change the Master PIN.

You can reset your PIN after successful verification. Once you hit the button "Continue" below, you will receive an automated telephone call at the mobile number, you have provided us with during the registration. A robot will communicate you a code to be typed in on the website and to be confirmed.

Change Auth Method

  1. Text-message Authentication. An Authentication Key will be sent to your mobile phone (verified during your registration with us) every time you make a payment or any other important operation on your account.
  2. Master PIN Authentication. An Authentication Key will be sent to your e-mail address (verified during your registration with us) every time you make a payment or any other important operation on your account. To confirm the operation you will have to input the Authentication Key from the email AND the Master PIN.

Please, choose your prefered Authentication method:

  1. Text-message Authentication
  2. Master PIN Authentication
